A Family Board Gaming Ranch

We specialize in 3+ night board gaming vacations for groups of up to 20 people. Thousands of board games and various outdoor activities ensure you will have a memorable vacation.

Fun on the Lake

Enjoy a range of watersports from swimming, kayaking, and paddle boarding. Also be sure to try out our water trampoline.

Explore the Outdoors

Hike, bike, or ride around our many forest trails. We provide group tours on a number of large and small gas powered side by side UTVs for the adults and young adults alike.

At Your Service

Onsite staff are ready to assist you in anything from learning a new board game, setting up activities or planning off site visits.

RC Cars & Planes

Challenge your friends to a race around our RC car track or see who can crawl to the top of our rockpile faster.

Fishing

Catch your next monster bass, crappie or bluegill from our fully stocked lake. There's nothing better than a fish fry after a day of gaming and fishing.

Indoors at the Ranch

You'll find something for everyone at Gamers Ranch.

Comfortably sleeps twenty guests in beds. Our four bedrooms and five full baths effortlessly accommodates four families. For the kids we have two sets of full bunk beds along with two additional full beds in the loft.

Over 1,000 board games to play. Find your perfect board game from our collection of over a thousand games. We also have many hard to find board games such as Star Wars Queen's Gambit, Starcraft, and Heroscape.

Playstation Pros in every room. You will find HDR TVs, Playstation Pros, and Google Chromecasts throughout Gamers Ranch. Put a party together to explore the depths of Minecraft or cast your favorite movies from Netflix on your rooms TV.

Indoor basketball and ping pong. Shoot some hoops and take on your friends on our indoor half court basketball area. If you want something a little faster paced, play singles or double on our indoor ping pong table.

Explore the Outdoors

You'll find something for everyone at Gamers Ranch.

Enjoy swimming, kayaking and more. Start by diving into the water from our dive platforms then race your friends in kayaks to see who can get to our floating island first. Finish it off by sunning yourself in the middle of the lake away from everything.

Miles of wooded trails. Head into the woods and see what adventure awaits. Explore the trails that wind through the property going over creeks and cross game trails. Find secluded sites, hidden areas, geocaches and more.

Fish our 11 acre stocked lake. The centerpiece of Gamers Ranch is our 11 acre stocked lake. Our fish program started in early 2017 and by 2018 we should have some nice size fish for you to catch.

Try golfing both ways. Try your hand at chipping golf balls across the lake and onto the peninsula. If standard gold isn't your game then try your hand at our nine hole frisbee golf course.
