A Family Board Gaming Ranch

We specialize in 3+ day board gaming vacations for groups of up to twenty people. Our unique blend of over a thousand games, beautiful view, outdoor activities and helpfull staff ensure you have a great vacation.

Game in Comfort

Enjoy our 8,400 sqft facility which sleeps twenty guests and features basketball, table tennis, every modern gaming console, a full kitchen, and six full baths.

Explore our 167 Acres

Get outside and swim, kayak, canoe or fish our 11 acre stocked lake. When you're done explore the ranch by hiking or riding ATVs around our 167 acres of forested trails.

Building a Gaming Paradise

  • Picking the Perfect Spot

    Located less than two hours drive east of St. Louis we are nestled in some of the most secluded and scenic parts of the Midwest. We are just a few miles away from the Gasconade River, Rock Island Spur trails, and a number of wineries.

  • Clearing the Land

    What started as 167 acres of hardwood and cedar trees has been transformed into an outdoor park of trails, ponds, campgrounds, and event areas. There is plenty to explore and new things to see every time you visit.

  • A Lake is Born

    The centerpiece of Gamers Ranch is our 11 acre stocked lake. It's giant play area for water sports, lounging, tubing and more. Our fish program started in early 2017 and by 2018 we should have some nice size fish for you to catch.

  • Construction Begins

    We broke ground on our 8,400 sq-ft event center / bed and breakfast in early 2017. The five bedroom and six bath structure has plenty of room to accommodate up to four families.
